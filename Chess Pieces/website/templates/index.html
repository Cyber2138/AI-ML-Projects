Introduction

Chess piece detection is crucial for automating game analysis and enhancing the chess experience. By using computer vision and artificial intelligence, systems can accurately recognize and classify each piece based on its unique shape and visual features. Advanced deep learning models, like Convolutional Neural Networks (CNNs), analyze images or video feeds of chess boards to identify pieces in real time, even under varied conditions. This technology not only improves strategic analysis and move tracking but also streamlines game recording, making it easier for players to review matches and enhance their skills, while providing a seamless blend of physical and digital chess play.
